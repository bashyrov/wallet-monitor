03 · Arb detail

A terminal for one pair. Charts, books, P&L, alerts.

Dual orderbooks side-by-side at 150ms cadence. Entry/Exit candlesticks that show the fill-adjusted divergence for your actual size. Funding forecast, next settlement, open interest, and a history of spreads back to page-load.

Size-aware divergence — walk the book to VWAP, so a $100k entry isn't priced like a tick.
Pair detection — Avalant spots your open two-leg position and shows combined P&L automatically.
Local history — 20 min of divergence preserved in the browser so the chart never starts from scratch.

08 · Security

Your keys. Your orders.
Our job to disappear.

Fernet-encrypted keys

API keys and signer private keys are PBKDF2-derived Fernet-encrypted at rest. Decrypted per-request, never logged, never serialized to disk outside of ciphertext.

Read-only by default

Connect with balance-only keys to monitor portfolios. Trade permission is an explicit opt-in — an account can be read-only across all venues if you want.

No custody, ever

Avalant never holds customer funds. All orders go directly to the venue via its native API — no broker layer, no intermediate wallet, no withdrawal rights.

Reduce-only closes

Every close instruction carries the exchange's explicit reduce flag — positions can never silently flip or open an opposing leg in hedge mode.

Rate-limited auth

Per-IP 10-req/60s rate limit on all authentication endpoints. Blocked users cannot session, even with a valid token. HttpOnly cookies protect pages server-side.

Provenance & open architecture

We publish our adapter code, changelog, and freshness-SLA telemetry. You audit what we audit — no black-box order router between you and the market.
