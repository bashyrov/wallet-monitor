      FAQ

      What payment methods do you accept?

      USDT / BTC / ETH and a few other coins via CryptoCloud — pick one at checkout, send the exact amount, plan activates within minutes of confirmation.

      How does the portfolio limit work?

      You can save unlimited keys, but only the wallets you tag as "Portfolio" count against your plan limit (5 on Free, 30 on Full). Switching a key from Portfolio to Screener-only frees a slot.

      What happens after my plan expires?

      Your data stays put. The portfolio-balance scan drops back to 5 wallets and trading orders run with the Free-tier 500 ms delay until you renew — but your wallets and history remain visible.

      Why pick Screener-only?

      It saves you $10/mo if all you want is the live arb scanner + trading. The Portfolio tracker is the difference between Screener-only and Full.

      Are promo codes stackable?

      No — exactly one code per checkout. Codes apply to the cart total (after the period discount) and round to two decimals.

      Can I switch plans?

      Yes. Upgrade anytime — your remaining time prorates onto the new tier. Downgrades take effect at the next billing date.
