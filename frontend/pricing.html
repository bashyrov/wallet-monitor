FAQ

What payment methods do you accept?

USDT / BTC / ETH and a few other coins via CryptoCloud — pick one at checkout, send the exact amount, plan activates within minutes of confirmation.

How does the portfolio limit work?

You can save unlimited keys, but only the wallets you tag as "Portfolio" count against your plan limit (5 on Free, 30 on paid). Switching a key from Portfolio to Screener-only frees a slot.

What happens after my plan expires?

Your data stays put. The portfolio-balance scan drops back to 5 wallets and trading orders run with the Free-tier 500 ms delay until you renew — but your wallets and history remain visible.

Why only 1 API key per exchange on Free?

Paid plans let you store up to 3 keys per venue and pick which one is "main" for trading — handy if you keep a separate read-only key. Free covers the basics with one key per exchange.

Are promo codes stackable?

No — exactly one code per checkout. Codes apply to the plan's base price (annual or monthly) before tax and round to two decimals.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. Upgrade anytime — your remaining time prorates onto the new tier. Downgrades take effect at the next billing date.
