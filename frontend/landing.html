How it works

Three steps. That's the whole thing.

You don't need to run our infrastructure or learn a new keyboard. Everything below is live the moment you sign up.

01
Connect — or don't.

Use the screener without any keys at all. Watch funding, spreads, watchlists. Ready to trade? Add a read-only key for position tracking, or a trading key for one-click execution. All credentials encrypted at rest with per-user Fernet keys.

02
Pick your spread.

Sort the arb table by net APR, click through to a pair you like. Live entry/exit candlestick chart tells you whether the spread is widening or compressing. Size-aware slippage simulator shows exactly what you'd get filled at.

03
Enter, hold, close.

Open both legs in parallel. Positions auto-pair by symbol and mirror size. Funding P&L accumulates across each venue's native history. One "Close Both" button reduces-only on both sides at market.

Security

Your keys stay yours.
We never see them.

API credentials are encrypted with a Fernet key derived from your account via PBKDF2-SHA256. The database stores ciphertext only; the key never touches logs, metrics, or third-party services. When you delete a wallet, the row is gone — not just flagged.

Read-only keys are limited to balance + position queries. Trading keys are opt-in per wallet and never stored in the same credential set as withdrawal permissions. HTTPS everywhere. HTTP-only cookies for session. No third-party analytics.
